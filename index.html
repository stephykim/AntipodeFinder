Antipode Finder

Where in the world is my opposite location?

The antipode of a location on Earth is the location diametrically opposite to it. Imagine if you were to put a straight line starting at your location, going through the center of the Earth. The end point at the opposite position on the globe would be your position's antipode. To find the antipode of a location we use the following calculations:

antipodeLatitude = -(currentLatitude)

antipodeLongitude = 180 - |currentLongitude|
